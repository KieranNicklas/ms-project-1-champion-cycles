The Benefits of Cycling

Mental Health

Riding a bike is relaxing. If you’re stressed at work, you can often burn it off by cycling home. And if you’re dealing with a more serious problem, cycling can help you regain some balance in your life.

Strengthen the Immune System

Numerous studies into exercise and immunity show that regular, modest cycling boosts immunity. In general, the fitter you are, the stronger your immune system.

Reduce the risk of Heart Disease

Cycling raises your heart rate and gets the blood pumping round your body, and it burns calories, limiting the chance of your being overweight.
