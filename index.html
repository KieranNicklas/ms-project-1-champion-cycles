Why Lease?

Bicylce leasing is a cost effective and flexible way of choosing a quality bike to match your cycling desire. Each bike is covered under our Capital Cover, so if an incident prevents you from being on the seat, we can help you out.

Is Leasing For Me?

Leasing provides people with the opportunity to change bikes as and when they choose. For exmaple, you would have the option of returning a Mountain bike in exchange for a Road bike with the minimal of fuss.

Why Us?

With a great range of a variety of bicycles on offer, you can have the pick of the bunch! We provide free delivery on a day of your choosing, or you can pop in store to collect your racer.
